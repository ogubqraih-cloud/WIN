凪の間 -nagi-no-ma-

When sunset becomes the chair.

When sunset becomes the chair.

茶と酒と、夕陽の時間。Tea, sake, and the hour of sunset.

凪の間は、西向きの大きなラウンジ。Nagi-no-ma is a large west-facing lounge.

日中は瀬戸内の茶葉と、島の柑橘で淹れる紅茶。夕方からは、岡山と広島の地酒、瀬戸内の蒸留酒。夜は焚き火と、短いつまみ──塩、燻製、柑橘、チョコレート。By day, tea leaves of the Seto and black tea infused with island citrus. From evening, local sake of Okayama and Hiroshima, and Seto distillates. At night, a wood fire and small bites — salt, smoked things, citrus, chocolate.

時間ごとに景色も飲み物も変わる、漣の中央広間です。As the hours change, so do the view and the drink. The central hall of Sazanami.
