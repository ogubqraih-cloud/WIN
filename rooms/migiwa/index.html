The Migiwa

汀に立つ、ふたりの部屋。A room for two, standing at the water edge.

汀は、海側に並ぶ45㎡のデラックス。The Migiwa rooms are 45 sqm deluxe rooms lining the sea-facing side.

静かなツインルーム、内湯。夕暮れ時、水面の光が天井に届きます。Quiet twin rooms, with an indoor bath. At dusk, light from the water reaches the ceiling.

Specifications

部屋のしつらえRoom appointments

汀は8室。本館南棟の1階・2階に分散して配置されます。海側に大きな開口、室内は45㎡の長方形。ふたりで過ごす時間を、もっとも素直に組み立てる部屋です。Eight Migiwa rooms are distributed across the first and second floors of the main south wing. Wide openings face the sea; the 45 sqm interior is a simple rectangle — a room that frames time spent together in the most straightforward way.
